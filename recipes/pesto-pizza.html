Pesto Pizza

Description

A tasty pizza using pesto sauce.

This recipe does not contain additional toppings. Add any toppings as you wish (chicken is a great choice)!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • Â½ cup pesto
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Spread pesto on pizza crust. Add any desired toppings.
  3. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.
